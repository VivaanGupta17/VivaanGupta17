### About Me

Biomedical engineering student at Johns Hopkins with deep focus on applying deep learning to clinical workflows — from medical image segmentation and surgical video analysis to cardiac signal processing and drug discovery. I build end-to-end ML pipelines designed for regulated medical environments, with working knowledge of FDA SaMD frameworks, GMLP principles, and PCCP-compliant model lifecycle management.

My work sits at the intersection of **computer vision for medical imaging**, **clinical signal processing**, and **production ML systems** that meet the quality bar for deployment in real clinical settings.
